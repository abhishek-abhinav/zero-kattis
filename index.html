About Our Game

"Zero Kattis" is an another term used for Tic-Tac-Toe game. It is also known by several names like Zero Kata, Chhaki Shuna, XOXO etc. Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic paper-and-pencil game that is often played by two players. The game is played on a 3x3 grid, and the players take turns marking an empty cell with their designated symbol, which is typically "X" for one player and "O" for the other. The objective is to get three of their symbols in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally before the opponent does.


The game is simple and easy to learn, making it popular among people of all ages. Tic-Tac-Toe is often used as a teaching tool for basic game theory and strategy. Despite its simplicity, the game has a finite number of possible outcomes, and a perfect player will always either win or tie.
